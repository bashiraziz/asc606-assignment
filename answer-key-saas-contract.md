## Expected Extraction Output

### Performance Obligation 1 — Platform Subscription

| Field | Expected Answer |
|---|---|
| **Obligation Name** | Platform Subscription — NovaCRM cloud CRM access (50 users) |
| **Recognition Method** | Time-elapsed |
| **Allocated Transaction Price** | USD 180,000 |
| **Start Date** | January 1, 2026 |
| **End Date** | December 31, 2026 |
| **Period Revenue** | USD 15,000 per month (USD 180,000 ÷ 12) |

**Expected reasoning:** The customer simultaneously receives and consumes the benefit of platform access each day. The contract explicitly states this — "the platform is made available on a continuous basis throughout the term." This satisfies the over-time criterion under ASC 606-10-25-27(a). Straight-line is appropriate because access is uniform across all 12 months.

### Performance Obligation 2 — Implementation Services

| Field | Expected Answer |
|---|---|
| **Obligation Name** | Implementation Services — configuration, data migration, go-live support |
| **Recognition Method** | Cost-to-cost (input method) |
| **Allocated Transaction Price** | USD 60,000 |
| **Start Date** | January 1, 2026 |
| **End Date** | March 31, 2026 |

**Expected reasoning:** Implementation is a service performed over time where the customer controls the asset (the configured system) as it is built — the contractor's performance does not create an asset with an alternative use, and the contractor has an enforceable right to payment for performance completed to date. The contract explicitly specifies cost-to-cost measurement: "revenue recognition shall track the ratio of costs incurred to total estimated costs at completion." This is the input method under ASC 606-10-55-20.

**Note for graders:** Students who argue milestone or time-elapsed for implementation without citing the contract language should not receive full marks on reasoning. The contract is explicit.

### Performance Obligation 3 — Premium Support and Account Management

| Field | Expected Answer |
|---|---|
| **Obligation Name** | Premium Support and Account Management (post go-live) |
| **Recognition Method** | Time-elapsed |
| **Allocated Transaction Price** | USD 24,000 |
| **Start Date** | April 1, 2026 |
| **End Date** | December 31, 2026 |
| **Period Revenue** | USD 2,666.67 per month (USD 24,000 ÷ 9) |

**Expected reasoning:** Support services are delivered on a stand-ready basis — the customer consumes the benefit (access to help desk, account manager, patches) as it is provided each month. This is the same over-time criterion as the subscription. Straight-line over 9 months is appropriate.

**Critical distinction:** The start date is April 1, 2026 (post Go-Live Date), not January 1, 2026. Students who use January 1 as the start date for support have misread the contract and should be penalised on extraction accuracy.

## Grader Notes

- The contract is intentionally explicit on recognition methods (Articles 2.1, 2.2, 2.3 contain direct guidance). This tests whether students read the contract carefully and whether their LLM prompt retrieves the relevant clauses.
- A strong submission will note that the payment milestones in Article 2.2 (30/40/30) are billing events, not recognition events — revenue is recognized based on costs incurred, not on when invoices are raised.
- The SSP note in Article 4 states no reallocation is required. Students who perform an unnecessary reallocation calculation have not read this clause.
