MASTER SUBSCRIPTION AND SERVICES AGREEMENT

Agreement No.: MSA-2025-0047
Effective Date: January 1, 2026
Expiry Date: December 31, 2026

PARTIES

Vendor:   NovaCRM Inc., a Delaware corporation ("Company")
Customer: Hartwell & Associates LLP, a professional services firm ("Customer")

---

RECITALS

This Master Subscription and Services Agreement ("Agreement") sets out the terms under which
NovaCRM Inc. will provide software subscription access, implementation services, and ongoing
technical support to Hartwell & Associates LLP during the term set out above.

---

ARTICLE 1 — DEFINITIONS

1.1 "Subscription" means the right to access and use the NovaCRM cloud-based CRM platform
    via the internet during the Subscription Term.

1.2 "Implementation Services" means the configuration, data migration, workflow setup, and
    go-live support activities described in Schedule A.

1.3 "Support Services" means the post-go-live help desk, bug resolution, and account
    management services described in Schedule B.

1.4 "Go-Live Date" means the date on which the Customer's production environment is
    activated, targeted for March 31, 2026.

---

ARTICLE 2 — PERFORMANCE OBLIGATIONS

2.1  PLATFORM SUBSCRIPTION

     The Company grants Customer a non-exclusive, non-transferable right to access the
     NovaCRM platform for up to 50 named users during the Subscription Term.

     Subscription Term: January 1, 2026 through December 31, 2026 (12 months)
     Subscription Fee: USD 180,000 payable in equal monthly instalments of USD 15,000
     on the first business day of each month.

     The platform is made available on a continuous basis throughout the term. Customer
     receives and consumes the benefit of platform access as it is provided each day.

2.2  IMPLEMENTATION SERVICES

     The Company will perform the implementation activities set out in Schedule A,
     estimated at 400 hours of professional services effort.

     Implementation Fee: USD 60,000 (fixed price)
     Estimated Timeline: January 1, 2026 through March 31, 2026 (3 months)

     Revenue recognition for implementation services shall track the ratio of costs
     incurred to total estimated costs at completion. The Company's project management
     office will update estimated costs to complete on a monthly basis.

     Payment: 30% on signing (USD 18,000); 40% at mid-point review (USD 24,000);
     30% on Go-Live Date (USD 18,000).

2.3  PREMIUM SUPPORT AND ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT

     Following the Go-Live Date, the Company will provide the support services described
     in Schedule B for the remainder of the Subscription Term.

     Support Fee: USD 24,000 for the support period April 1, 2026 through December 31, 2026
     (9 months), payable in equal monthly instalments of USD 2,666.67.

     Support services are delivered continuously and consumed by the Customer as provided.

---

ARTICLE 3 — TOTAL CONTRACT VALUE

     Platform Subscription:       USD 180,000
     Implementation Services:     USD  60,000
     Premium Support:             USD  24,000
                                  -----------
     Total Contract Value:        USD 264,000

---

ARTICLE 4 — STANDALONE SELLING PRICES

     The Company establishes standalone selling prices ("SSPs") annually based on
     observable transactions with similar customers:

     Platform Subscription (50 users, 12 months):  USD 180,000
     Implementation Services (400 hours at $150):  USD  60,000
     Premium Support (9 months):                   USD  24,000

     No reallocation is required as the sum of SSPs equals the total contract price.

---

ARTICLE 5 — PAYMENT TERMS

5.1  Subscription fees are invoiced monthly in advance.
5.2  Implementation fees are invoiced per the milestone schedule in Article 2.2.
5.3  Support fees are invoiced monthly in advance from April 1, 2026.
5.4  Payment is due net 30 days from invoice date.
5.5  Late payments accrue interest at 1.5% per month.

---

ARTICLE 6 — INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

6.1  The platform and all underlying software remain the exclusive property of NovaCRM Inc.
6.2  Customer data remains the exclusive property of Hartwell & Associates LLP.
6.3  Any custom configurations or workflows developed during Implementation Services
     become the property of the Customer upon full payment.

---

ARTICLE 7 — TERMINATION

7.1  Either party may terminate for material breach with 30 days written notice if
     the breach is not cured within that period.
7.2  The Customer may not terminate the Subscription for convenience during the term.
7.3  Upon termination, any prepaid and unearned fees shall be refunded pro-rata.

---

ARTICLE 8 — GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware, United States.

---

SCHEDULE A — IMPLEMENTATION SCOPE

Phase 1 — Discovery and Configuration (January 2026)
  - Requirements workshop (2 days)
  - System configuration: pipelines, fields, user roles
  - Integration with Customer's existing billing system

Phase 2 — Data Migration and Testing (February 2026)
  - Historical CRM data migration (up to 50,000 records)
  - User acceptance testing (UAT) — 2 rounds
  - Performance and security testing

Phase 3 — Training and Go-Live (March 2026)
  - End-user training (4 sessions, up to 50 users)
  - Go-live support (5 business days on-site)
  - Handover documentation

---

SCHEDULE B — SUPPORT SERVICES (POST GO-LIVE)

  - Help desk: business hours, response within 4 hours for P1 issues
  - Monthly account review call
  - Platform updates and patches (included, no additional charge)
  - Named account manager assigned to the Customer
